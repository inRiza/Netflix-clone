The Batman

4.5 ★★★★☆

In the gritty, rain-soaked streets of Gotham City, The Batman (2022), directed by Matt Reeves, delivers a dark, noir-driven take on the iconic DC hero. Robert Pattinson stars as a young, brooding Bruce Wayne, two years into his vigilante crusade as Batman, driven by vengeance to rid the city of crime.
